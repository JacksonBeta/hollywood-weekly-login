About Hollywood Weekly Platform

The Hollywood Weekly Distribution Platform is a comprehensive solution for independent filmmakers to distribute their content to major streaming services including Google TV, Prime Video, Peacock, and Apple TV.

Global Distribution

Reach millions of viewers worldwide through our partnerships with major streaming platforms.

Revenue Generation

Monetize your content through multiple revenue models including subscription, pay-per-view, and advertising.

Performance Analytics

Track your content's performance and revenue in real-time across all platforms.

Copyright Protection

Secure your intellectual property with our integrated copyright registration system.

Platform Features

Our platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to help filmmakers distribute, monetize, and track their content.

One-Click Distribution

Upload your content once and distribute it to multiple streaming platforms simultaneously.

Multiple Revenue Models

Choose from subscription (SVOD), pay-per-view (TVOD), or ad-supported (AVOD) revenue models.

Advanced Analytics

Access detailed analytics dashboards showing views, engagement, and revenue metrics.

Monthly Statements

Receive automated monthly revenue statements with platform-specific breakdowns.

Promotional Tools

Create trailers, promotional clips, and marketing materials for your content.

Audience Insights

Understand your audience demographics and viewing patterns to optimize your content.
