Distribution Process

1
Upload Your Film
Upload your film in high quality (minimum 1080p). We accept various formats including MP4, MOV, and ProRes. Include all required metadata and marketing materials.
2
Select Platforms & Monetization
Choose which platforms you want to distribute to and select your monetization strategy (SVOD, TVOD, AVOD). You can use different strategies for different platforms.
3
Review & Approval
Our team will review your submission to ensure it meets all platform requirements. This typically takes 2-3 business days. We'll provide feedback if any changes are needed.
4
Distribution
Once approved, we'll submit your film to the selected platforms. Platform review typically takes 1-2 weeks. You'll receive notifications at each stage of the process.
5
Track Performance & Receive Revenue
Monitor your film's performance across all platforms from your dashboard. Revenue is paid monthly with detailed statements. Our platform fee is just 15% of generated revenue.
